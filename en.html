10 Minutes to Isshiki Beach.
BBQ on the Rooftop Terrace.

From the terrace, watch the ever-changing sky. Stroll through charming lanes, relax on the Imperial Villa's quiet beach, and witness Mt. Fuji glowing at sunset during the magical hour. Pack a camping chair for a beach picnic, or enjoy a BBQ on the third-floor terrace.

Supermarkets, convenience stores, and restaurants are all within walking distance.

A Rooftop Terrace Living Room
for a Leisurely Holiday

The third-floor terrace and living area are the most relaxing spots in the house. Sip your morning coffee with the sunrise, or share conversation with loved ones while the evening sky transforms in color. The terrace BBQ is a guest favorite.

Your Perfect Base for Kamakura, Shonan & the Miura Peninsula

Just one train stop from Kamakura. Take a coastal drive to Enoshima, shop for fresh fish at Sajima Fishing Port and cook it yourself, or visit Soleil Hill and Jogashima Island. An excellent base for fishing or diving trips as well.

*From Zushi Station, take the local bus (approx. 15 minutes) to reach the house.
